About IKDS

Founded in 2013 by Wilbert Bean III, I Know Development Solutions (IKDS) main goal has been to provide businesses with a vast array of IT services at the best price, in a simple and easy to understand package.

Four years later and IKDS still applies these principles to clients across the TriState. Unlike a waste removal company, IKDS is committed to low prices and making waste disposal services a simple and easy process as well as offering unmatched attachment services.

No more waiting, questioning whether or not you will be serviced, or dealing with unexpected changes in service. IKDS takes away all the hassle, time, and complication out of your waste services.
