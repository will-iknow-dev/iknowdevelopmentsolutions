At I Know Development Solutions we confident that we will provide affordable, highly efficient Computer Refurbishment and Recycling management programs to businesses throughout the TriState Area.

When you work with I Know Development Solutions, you’ll immediately benefit from our in-depth knowledge of waste and recycling resources, and our commitment to hands-on customer service.
